## What This Lab Was About

---

## What I Learned

Neurons communicate through electrical signals. A neuron receives inputs through its dendrites, integrates them in the cell body, and fires an action potential down the axon when the signal is strong enough to cross the threshold. That basic process — integrate, threshold, fire — is the same pattern that inspired artificial neurons.

The key takeaway from this lab was understanding that AI is not just loosely inspired by biology. The specific mechanics of how neurons process inputs map directly onto how artificial neural networks are structured.

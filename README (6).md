## About Me

My name is Judith Barrios. I'm a student at Houston City College. I took this course because I wanted to understand where AI actually comes from — not just how to use it, but why it works the way it does and what biological systems inspired it.

This course made me look at the brain not just as something scientists study, but as a design reference. The way neurons fire, the way attention works, the way memory forms — all of it connects to decisions made in how AI systems are built. That connection is what this portfolio is about.

## Things I Took Away From This Course

- The brain is not just a biology topic — it is a design reference for AI
- Attention, memory, and emotion in AI are not just metaphors, they map to real architectural choices
- ChatGPT can sound very human without actually being brain-like in the ways that matter most
- Small changes in neuron parameters like threshold or time constant completely change how a system behaves — and the same is true in AI
